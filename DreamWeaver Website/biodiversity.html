Scrubland Green Oasis- Marginal lands provide shelter and food Bramble shrub supports:

  1. Blackbirds
  2. Thrushes
  3. Chaffinches
  4. Starlings
  5. Robins
  6. Foxes
  7. Mice
  8. And other small mammals eat fruits.
It acts as a cover for foxes, and growsflowers from May to September, and grows fruit from August to October.
